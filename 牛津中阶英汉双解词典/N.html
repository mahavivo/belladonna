*need¹
/ni:d ||; nid/verb [T] (not usually used in the continuous tenses 通常不用于进行时)

The question form of the main verb need is do I need ?, etc and the past tense is needed (question form did you need ?, etc; negative didn't need). 主要动词need的疑问式是do I need?，而过去时是needed（疑问式是did you need?，否定式是didn't need）。
3 need (sth) doing if sth needs doing, it is necessary or must be done 须要或必要办到（某事）: This jumper needs washing. 这件套头毛衣要洗了。 He needed his eyes testing. 他须要做眼睛检查。
